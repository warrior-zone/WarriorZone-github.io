Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 22 March 2026

This policy explains when refunds may or may not be issued on WarriorZone. By participating in tournaments, you agree to this policy.

1. Entry Fee Refunds

Entry fees are non-refundable once a player joins a tournament.

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Losing a match
  • Not joining the room on time
  • Leaving the match voluntarily
  • Poor internet or device performance
  • Disqualification due to rule violation
  • Incorrect game ID submission

2. When Refunds Are Provided

Refunds are issued only in the following cases:

A. Match Cancelled by WarriorZone

If a tournament is cancelled due to server issues, insufficient players, technical problems, or admin decision, the entry fee will be fully refunded to wallet.

B. Match Not Started / Room Not Released

If room ID & password are not shared before match time, full refund will be issued.

C. Duplicate Payment

If a user is charged twice for the same match, the extra payment will be refunded.

3. Refund Method

All refunds are credited to the WarriorZone wallet.

Wallet refunds:

  • Can be used to join future matches
  • Can be withdrawn after meeting withdrawal conditions

No bank refunds are provided for entry fees.

4. Withdrawal Fees

Withdrawal processing fees (if any) are non-refundable once processed.

5. Processing Time

Eligible refunds are processed within 24–72 hours.

6. Fraud & Abuse

WarriorZone reserves the right to deny refunds if:

  • Fraudulent activity is detected
  • Misuse of refund requests occurs
  • Multiple suspicious claims are made

7. Policy Updates

WarriorZone may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the platform means acceptance of updates.

8. Contact Us

For refund-related queries:

Email: support.warriorzone@gmail.com

Response Time: Within 24–48 hours

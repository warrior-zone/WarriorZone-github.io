Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

WarriorZone respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name / Username
  • Email address
  • Firebase UID
  • Player ID / Profile details

Financial Information:

  • UPI ID / Bank details
  • Transaction history
  • Withdrawal requests

Usage Data:

  • Matches joined
  • Wins and leaderboard stats
  • App activity
  • Device and OS information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Allow tournament participation
  • Process wallet transactions
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Display leaderboard rankings
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect fraud and cheating
  • Improve platform performance

We do NOT sell your personal data.

3. Public Information

The following may be visible to other users:

  • Username
  • Leaderboard rank
  • Wins and match stats

We never display sensitive data like email or bank details publicly.

4. Data Sharing

Firebase (Google):

Used for authentication, database, and notifications.

Payment Providers:

Used for deposits and withdrawals. Only necessary data is shared.

We do not share data beyond what is required to operate the platform.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Access control rules

However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Notifications

With your permission, we may send:

  • Match reminders
  • Results and updates
  • Important announcements

You can disable notifications in device settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

WarriorZone is not intended for users under 18 years. Accounts found to belong to minors will be removed.

8. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as the account is active or required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention
  • Financial records

9. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your account

To request deletion, contact support.

10. Account Deletion

You can request permanent account deletion via email.

Wallet balance must be withdrawn before deletion. Processing may take up to 7 days.

11. Third-Party Links

The platform may contain links to external services such as game publishers or payment gateways. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform means acceptance of updates.
